ENHANCED COMMANDS
-----------------
ps aux | wc -l  --> shows the count of running process
ps aux 		--> shows the running process ids and information

ps aux | head -n 5 --> shows the top 5 running process id and information
ps aux | tail -n 5 --> shows the bottom 5 running process id and information

ps aux | head -n 5 | awk '{print $4}' --> shows the top 5 running process's 4 column only

ps aux | head -n 5 | awk '$4 > 0.2'  --> shows the value more than 0.2 in the 4th column of top 5 process

ps -eo pid,user,cmd --> shows the running process ids, users, cmts
ps -eo pid,user,cmd |head -n 5 --> shows the running process ids, users, cmts in top 5

ps -eo pid,user,cmd |tail -n 5 > demo.txt --> print the running process ids, users, cmts of bottom 5 in demo.txt file

ps -eo pid,user,cmd,%mem --sort=-%mem | head -n 10 --> shows the running process ids, users, mem and cmts of top 10
